## 📐 Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High resolution (not pixelated)
- **Compression**: Optimized for web (under 500KB each)
- **Aspect Ratios**:
  - Hero: 16:9 (landscape)
  - Portraits: 3:4 or 1:1
  - Training: 4:3 or 16:9

### Style Guide:
- **Lighting**: Natural, bright, motivational
- **Colors**: Vibrant but realistic
- **Subjects**: Clear faces, genuine emotion
- **Background**: Outdoor preferred (tracks, parks, roads)
- **Avoid**: Stock photo "fake" look, studio only
