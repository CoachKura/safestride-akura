The 6 Pillars of Injury Prevention

Our comprehensive assessment evaluates every aspect of your running biomechanics

01

Alignment

Q-angle, foot pronation, pelvic drop, and postural assessment

02

Running Capacity

VO2 max, 5K performance, weekly mileage, and endurance metrics

03

Strength

Lower body, core stability, and functional strength testing

04

Balance

Single-leg stance, dynamic stability, and proprioception

05

Mobility

Hip, ankle, and hamstring flexibility and range of motion

06

Recovery

Sleep quality, stress levels, and injury history analysis
