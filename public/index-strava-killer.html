Everything You Need to Run Injury-Free

AI-powered features that keep you training smart

AI AISRI Score

Get your personalized Injury Risk Score (0-100) based on 6 scientific pillars: Mobility, Strength, Balance, ROM, Alignment, Running Performance.

Safety Gates

AI prevents overtraining by locking high-intensity zones until your AISRI score improves. Train hard, but train safe.

Progress Tracking

Visualize your AISRI evolution, km milestones (0→500→1000→5000), and health metrics over time.

Physical Assessments

Monthly ROM, strength, balance, and mobility tests with image capture. Track improvements scientifically.

12-Week Training Plans

Progressive programs with 7 core protocols: START, ENGINE, OXYGEN, POWER, ZONES, STRENGTH, LONG RUN.

Health Reversal Tracking

Monitor diabetes (HbA1c), hypertension (BP), cholesterol, and weight loss. Reverse conditions through running.

Strava Integration

Auto-sync activities from Strava. Keep using your favorite app, get injury prevention insights.

Coach Dashboard

Professional coaches can monitor multiple athletes, assign plans, and track progress in real-time.

Leaderboards & Challenges
