Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Visit SafeStride App, click "Sign Up", and verify your email.

2. Take AISRI Assessment (10 min)

This evaluates your fitness level and ensures safe workout recommendations.

Tips:

  • Be honest about your current fitness
  • Choose conservative options if unsure
  • Don't inflate your abilities

3. Explore Power Cells

Your personalized workout library:

  • 🟢 Available - Ready for you
  • 🔒 Locked - Unlock as you improve
  • ✅ Completed - Your history

4. Schedule Your First Workout

Pick an available cell, click "Schedule", and start your journey!

⚠️ Safety First

Stop and rest if you experience:

  • Sharp pain (not normal muscle fatigue)
  • Dizziness or nausea
  • Chest pain or breathing difficulty
  • Joint pain that changes your form

Remember: It's okay to skip workouts, take extra rest, or repeat easier cells!
