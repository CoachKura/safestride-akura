Your Journey

How SafeStride Works

Four simple steps to injury-free performance. Our AI-powered system guides you every step of the way.

1. Complete AISRI Assessment

Take our comprehensive 6-pillar evaluation: Mobility, Strength, Mental Resilience, Recovery, Injury Prevention, and Performance. Connect your Strava to analyze your complete running history and get your personalized AISRI score (0-100).

2. Get Safety Gates Assigned

Based on your AISRI score, we assign training zones you can safely access. AISRI 42? Start with Zone 1-2 only. As you improve, new zones unlock automatically and help prevent 85% of running injuries.

3. Follow Your Dynamic Plan

Receive a 12-week training plan with START, ENGINE, OXYGEN, POWER, ZONES, STRENGTH, LONG RUN. Your plan adapts if you miss workouts, and Coach Kura monitors your progress daily.

4. Achieve Your Goals Injury-Free

Monthly re-evaluations track your improvement and unlock new training zones as your AISRI score increases. Train like a pro, perform like an elite athlete, and keep progressing without injuries.
