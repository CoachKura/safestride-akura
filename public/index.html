The 7 Core Protocols

Every Week, Perfectly Balanced

Seven evidence-based training protocols that rotate throughout your week — optimizing every energy system while preventing overtraining.

START

Easy recovery runs at conversational pace. Build aerobic base and active recovery between hard sessions.

ENGINE

Steady-state aerobic runs at moderate intensity. Improve endurance and fat-burning efficiency.

OXYGEN

VO2 max intervals at 95-100% effort. Increase maximum oxygen uptake and cardiovascular capacity.

POWER

Short explosive intervals and hill sprints. Develop speed, power, and neuromuscular coordination.

ZONES

Progressive zone training based on your AISRI score. Unlock faster paces as you improve.

STRENGTH

Running-specific strength training. Build resilience in muscles, tendons, and joints.

LONG RUN

Weekly long run at easy pace. Build endurance and mental toughness for race day.
