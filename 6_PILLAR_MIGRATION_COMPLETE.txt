🧮 NEW AISRI FORMULA
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

AISRI = (MF + CS + MR + RR + IP + PO) / 6

Where:
- MF = Mobility & Flexibility (0-100)
- CS = Core Strength & Stability (0-100)
- MR = Mental Resilience (0-100)
- RR = Recovery & Regeneration (0-100)
- IP = Injury Prevention (0-100)
- PO = Performance Optimization (0-100)

Result: AISRI Score (0-100)

Risk Categories:
- 0-39:   Critical Risk (Zone 1 Only)
- 40-59:  High Risk (Zones 1-2)
- 60-74:  Medium Risk (Zones 1-3)
- 75-100: Low Risk (All Zones 1-5)

A: Yes! Edit the formula in:
